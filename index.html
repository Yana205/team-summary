CandiHub — What It Is & What It Means for Us

Candivore (the studio behind Match Masters) runs a 3-month funded talent-development program for small game teams. Next cohort: October 2026 — applications are open now.

💰 3-month living stipend (flexible amount)
🧭 Mentorship from Candivore experts
🏢 Workspace in their Tel Aviv office
📜 No strings — the IP stays 100% yours
🔗 Program page
📝 Application form
💬 Program Discord

1What CandiHub Is Looking For

The team

  • 2–4 people (no solo devs) covering art + design + code
  • Independent, learn-by-doing, serious about game dev as a career

The project

  • #1 thing: a playable, testable core loop in 3 months
  • Clear gameplay + art direction, with commercial potential (not experimental art pieces)

The process

  • Apply → interviews → 3 months of dev → showcase to a professional panel
  • Periodic check-ins; at least one day/week at their Tel Aviv office; goals set together during acceptance

What they give

  • Living stipend, mentorship (design, dev, art + data, economy, marketing), workspace
  • Zero IP grab — what you make stays yours

2What This Means for Us

⚡ Bottom line: a funded 3-month sprint to a playable, commercially-minded prototype. The bar is a working core loop with a clear direction — not a finished game. Today we need to agree on: team (2–4), concept with a commercial hook, and what's playable at month 3. Lead the application with a playable mechanic and one strong visual identity — and if unsure about fit, they explicitly say: just talk to them (email / Discord).

6Commercial Benchmarks — Cozy Cat Genre

💡 The steal: Animal Restaurant's letter system is the cheapest retention per art-hour in the genre — a short text message gives every character a personality without animation or voice. Ship ~15 characters with letters, not 250 silent ones. The games below prove the market is real and the loop is tight.
🍵

Cats & Soup iOS

IdleCasualAndroid / iOS2021

Cats cook soup in a forest clearing. Tap to hire cats, assign recipes, upgrade pots. 50M+ installs on Google Play, 4.6★ (2M+ ratings). Developer: HIDEA.

Loop: place cat → recipe → collect coins → unlock cat. Zero friction, maximum cozy.
Signal: proves idle + cat identity + pixel art = 50M audience. No letters — retention is purely collection-driven.
🐰

Usagi Shima — petting iOS

IdlePettingAndroid / iOS2021

Bunny island where you leave treats, bunnies visit, you pet them. Closest mechanic to Neko Atsume with modern polish. Developer: NOX Games.

The "visit + pet" loop — passive, visit-triggered dopamine. No economy needed.
Signal: petting as the entire core mechanic. Audience will pay for access to cute characters doing nothing.
🐱

Neko Atsume iOS

CollectionIdleiOS / Android2014

The genre's origin. Leave food → cats visit → photograph → collect. 20M+ downloads. 80 cats, rare cats, seasonal events. Developer: Hit-Point. Cats leave "fish" as thank-you gifts — proto-letters.

Zero real-time interaction. The cats come to you.
Signal: patience + anticipation loop invented here. Proved idle cat games are a category, not a fluke.
🍜

Animal Restaurant iOS

Idle restaurantLettersAndroid / iOS2019

Run a restaurant for animal customers. Customers write you personal letters after eating — a paragraph of personality per character. 10M+ installs, 4.7★. Developer: Treeplla.

The letter system: one static sprite + one text file = a character players remember. Art budget: near zero. Retention: high.
The steal: copy this exactly. Letters cost one afternoon per character. They make players care more than a cutscene would.
🐟

Cat Snack Bar iOS

Idle restaurantAndroid / iOS2022

Cats run a snack bar. Serve customers, collect coins, upgrade the bar. 5M+ installs, 4.5★. Developer: Fluffy Fairy Games. More focused cast and tighter progression than its peers.

Fewer characters, deeper per-character content. The scoping lesson in a live product.
Signal: shipped 2022 into a crowded genre and won with restraint. 15 well-made cats beat 250 generic ones.
